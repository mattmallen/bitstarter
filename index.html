Have you ever been to an event and forgotten your camera? Have you or someone you know hired a subpar photographer for an event, only to find out afterwards? What if you could access all the pictures taken by other attendees? What if you could easily aggregate all of the photos taken by your guests? YAPS (yet another photo share) is here to help. We've been through the hassle of trying to track down pictures and find a way to transfer big chunks of data. We've used social networking sites to try to aggregate photos for a group, but it never keeps the full resolution. We have wanted a way to bring together photos from our friends and other event attendees but have never found the right soution for us.

To that end, we are building a service that will allow a group to easily aggregate their photos at full resolution into a single location to share with the group. The creator of an album will be able to control who sees the album, who can load pictures, and will be able to review all submissions. Rating, voting, and sharing will also be integrated to allow for contests and group-sourced scrapbooks.
